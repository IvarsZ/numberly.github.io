As a maths teacher, Numberly saves you time by streamlining the process of setting, collecting and marking work. We help you in every stage of the process, from finding worksheets to giving feedback:

Worksheets

We know how frustrating it can be if you can't find the right worksheet. With Numberly you can search worksheets made by other teachers and if it's not quite what you're looking for, edit it with our super-simple and quick editor. Numberly even lets you generate unique worksheets for each student by mixing-and-matching questions.

Hand-in

Don't waste time collecting in assignments and handing out feedback. Your students can submit their work online using a scanner or by dowloading our mobile app and using the phone camera, and receive their marks and feedback online.

Auto-marking

Numberly will automatically mark questions with numerical or single-word answers, saving you loads of time. We use cutting-edge handwriting recognition and machine learning to automatically mark the final answers of questions while still allowing you as a teacher to give partial marks for working and see where students went wrong.


About us

Numberly was founded by two maths gradudates, Ivars and Martin, who were convinced there was a better way for teachers to spend their time. A lot of marking can be menial and time-consuming work, but other solutions relied on students doing work on computers which don't show working or were limited to multiple choice questions.
