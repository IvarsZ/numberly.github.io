Worksheets

We know how frustrating it can be if you can't find the right worksheet. With Numberly you can search worksheets made by other teachers and if it's not quite what you're looking for, edit it with our super-simple and quick editor.

Hand-in

Save time when collecting in assignments and handing out feedback. Your students can submit their work online using a scanner or their phone. We'll work with you to find the best hand-in solution for your class.

Marking

We automatically mark simple questions, saving you loads of time. If your student gets it wrong, we'll let you know so that you can see their mistakes and give instant feedback. You don't have to carry papers around, so you can mark from anywhere.


About us

Numberly was founded by two maths gradudates, Ivars and Martin, who were convinced there was a better way for teachers to spend their time. A lot of marking can be menial and time-consuming work, but other solutions relied on students doing work on computers which don't show working or were limited to multiple choice questions.
