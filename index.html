Numberly saves time for maths teachers by streamlining the process of setting, collecting and marking work. We use cutting-edge handwriting recognition and machine learning to automatically mark the final answers of questions while still allowing you as a teacher to give partial marks for working and see where students went wrong.

We help you in every stage of the process, from finding worksheets to giving feedback:

Worksheets

Quickly find, edit and personalise worksheets in moments. Blah blah more text about worksheet creation. Make it sound great!

Hand-in

Don't waste time collecting in assignments. Your students can submit their work online using a scanner or by dowloading our mobile app and using the phone camera.

Auto-marking

Numberly will automatically mark questions with numerical or single-word answers, saving you loads of time. For the rest of the questions, a super-simple interface lets you give marks for working and explanations.

Numberly was founded by two Maths gradudates, Ivars and Martin, who were convinced there was a better way for teachers to spend their time. A lot of marking can be menial and time-consuming work, but other solutions relied on students doing work on computers which don't show working or were limited to multiple choice questions.
